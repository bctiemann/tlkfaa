Your Folders

Here you can set up folders to help organize your pictures. Each folder can have a name and a description, and folders can be placed inside other folders. Once you have created some folders, you can move pictures into them and upload new pictures directly into the folders where they should go.

If you delete a folder, the pictures in it are not deleted—they are just moved up one level into the parent folder.
