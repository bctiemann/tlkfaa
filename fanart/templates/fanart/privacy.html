TLKFAA Privacy Policy

What information do we collect?

Members of the TLKFAA may, at their option, choose to submit certain personally identifiable information to enhance the service provided by the site. This information may include the member's name, email address, occupation, location, birthdate, Instant Messaging contact methods, and other information furnished solely at the discretion of each member.

This information is collected from registered users of the site. Visitors to the site may, however, choose to browse anonymously, by not registering a profile.

What is this information used for?

Information provided by each member is used to help enhance the experience of the site, by facilitating community interaction such as posting art, commenting, and observing the details of other members such as birthdate and location. Members who "favorite" other artists or their pictures may be revealed as "fans" of those artists or pictures, though this information is disclosed only to the artist in question and is not displayed publicly.

We will never use or share any personally identifiable information collected from TLKFAA members for any form of mass marketing, service offerings, studies, or for any other reason except as may be required by law.

Will this Policy change?

We may revise this Privacy Policy at any time. Any material changes to the substance of the Policy will be announced here on this page.

This Policy page was last revised on December 25, 2010.
